1.2 TEXT FORMATTING

Like I said at the end of the last lesson, one of the first things you'll notice as you play around in this sandbox is that you're pretty harshly limited as far as your web page's appearance. Times New Roman. Black text. White background It's a timeless, classic look for a book go, but boring as fuck for a web page.

In order to do things like break text into paragraphs, bold, italicize, and underline, you have to use additional tags
